Our Story

We are four passionate college students from Alexandria, Egypt, united by a shared vision—to create a special website dedicated to our beloved city. Our name "Alexplore" combines "Alexandria" and "Explore"—a perfect reflection of our mission to guide you through the heart of our city. Our goal is to showcase Alexandria’s rich history, vibrant culture, and hidden gems while connecting locals and visitors with the best experiences the city has to offer. From historical landmarks to modern hotspots, we want to build a digital hub that celebrates Alexandria’s unique charm. Through this project, we hope to inspire pride in our community and make it easier for everyone to explore and fall in love with our coastal gem. Join us on this journey as we bring Alexandria to the world, one click at a time!
